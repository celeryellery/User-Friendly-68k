How do I find the ISBN?


1 Turn your book over and locate the bar code on the back cover (usually in the bottom right-hand corner).


2 Look for the long, hyphenated number at the top of the bar code.
This number might be labeled "ISBN." The number might begin with 978 or 979, but it can still be correct even if it begins with other numbers. The number will be either 10 digits or 13 digits long. Either the ISBN-10 or the ISBN-13 is acceptable. Do not enter dashes. Make sure you count the digits, as no other lengths are acceptable!


3 Ready to try it? Type this number into the ISBN box on the BookIt homepage!
